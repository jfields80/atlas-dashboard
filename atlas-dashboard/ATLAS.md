# Atlas Investment OS

Version: 1.0
Status: Active Development

---

# Mission

Atlas is an autonomous Investment Operating System.

Its purpose is to discover, evaluate, build, launch, monitor, and manage profitable digital businesses with minimal human intervention.

Atlas is not simply a website builder.

Atlas is an operating system for acquiring and creating cash-flow producing internet assets.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Atlas should eventually be capable of:

• Discovering profitable markets

• Evaluating competitors

• Finding underserved niches

• Estimating commercial opportunity

• Designing superior products

• Generating complete businesses

• Launching websites

• Monitoring performance

• Managing a portfolio of businesses

• Recommending acquisitions

• Forecasting ROI

---

# Core Principles

Everything in Atlas is:

- deterministic
- testable
- modular
- reproducible
- repository-driven

Business logic never belongs inside Flask.
